### Purpose of the GDGOC Unilorin Resource Hub

The GDGOC Unilorin Resource Hub is designed to **empower community members** by providing a centralized, curated collection of learning materials, guides, and tools. Its goal is to help you grow as a skilled, confident, and connected tech professional.
